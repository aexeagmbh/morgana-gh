What is ax? Ax is a Caas (Content-as-a-Service) software
that turns your data into stories.

The software is based on a highly sophisticated algorithm that identifies and interprets your data and
turns it into meaningful, personalized information. Available in up to 13 languages, AX is highly flexible
and scalable, and can be used in various areas, from news to sports to weather, to financial reports
and product information.

Five Reasons to use Ax

Grow your business faster

With AX you can produce more content and you can produce it quicker. Never worry again about writer’s block or late-night editing. You need to produce many stories fast? No problem. With AX, you get your data within a minute. Time to market has never been this fast before.

internationalize easier

You are already using AX in English and now you want to expand your business to other countries? No problem. Send us your data in the new language, and once we have configured your software, off you go. Never worry about hiring expensive internationals or paying big bucks to translation agencies again.

save more money

The more content you want, the more money you save. Think about it. How much would it cost to employ someone to write 1 million articles? And how much time would it take? Enough said. You save money if you let us do the writing.

never suffer bad writing

Still cringing from that last blatant spelling error you discovered on your website recently? Never again. Receive flawless content, always grammatically correct…only if you want, of course.

Set the tone and perfectly

You want all of your content to have the same voice? More importantly, you want to be consistent in language and tone? Consult with us and choose how you want to address your customers correctly. Never again allow room for misinterpretation.

 

How it works

  1. Contact us via mail, phone, or through our easy contact form.
  2. A member of our team will contact you within 48 hours and consult with you to determine your needs.
  3. Once you send us a sample of your data, we will review it and see what your data has to say. You will receive from us sample offerings to choose from.
  4. After you’ve chosen the type, we will implement for you a customized version of AX, making sure that throughout all customizations are to your liking.
  5. When you are happy and ready, AX is ready to go. Now you send us your data real-time and return to your personalized content within a minute.
